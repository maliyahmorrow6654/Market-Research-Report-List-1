<p><p>The Lithium Triborate Crystal market offers two types of products - Double Frequency and Triple Frequency. Double Frequency crystals are utilized in devices that require the conversion of a single input frequency into two output frequencies of equal amplitude. These crystals find applications in areas such as laser technologies and medical equipment. On the other hand, Triple Frequency crystals are used in devices that convert a single input frequency into three output frequencies of equal amplitude. These crystals find applications in areas like scientific research and military technologies.</p></p>
