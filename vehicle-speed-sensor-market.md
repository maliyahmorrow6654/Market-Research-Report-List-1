<p><strong>The Vehicle Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) market consists of two main types: Hall type and Magnetic Electric type. The Hall type VSS utilizes a Hall effect sensor to detect the vehicle's speed by measuring the changes in the magnetic field. This type is widely used due to its reliability, accuracy, and durability. On the other hand, the Magnetic Electric type VSS relies on a magnet and coil to generate an electric signal corresponding to the vehicle's speed. This type is known for its simplicity, low cost, and ease of installation.</p></p>
