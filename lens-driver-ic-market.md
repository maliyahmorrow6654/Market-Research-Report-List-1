<p><p>The Lens Driver IC market is categorized into different types based on the number of channels it supports. Single Channel Lens Driver ICs are designed for applications that require control of a single lens motor or coil. Dual Channel Lens Driver ICs can handle the control of two separate lens motors or coils simultaneously. Similarly, Four Channel Lens Driver ICs can control four lens motors or coils independently. These variations allow manufacturers to choose the appropriate Lens Driver IC based on their specific requirements and the complexity of the optical system.</p></p>
