<p><p>Laboratory thermostats are devices used to maintain precise temperature control in scientific research settings. They come in two main types: desktop and floor-standing models. Desktop laboratory thermostats are compact and designed to be placed on laboratory benches or countertops. They are suitable for small-scale experiments and provide accurate temperature regulation. On the other hand, floor-standing laboratory thermostats are larger and more robust, designed to handle large-scale experiments or multiple samples simultaneously. They are usually equipped with advanced features such as larger temperature ranges and more precise controls.</p></p>
