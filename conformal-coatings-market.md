<p><p>Conformal coatings are used to protect electronic components and circuit boards from environmental and mechanical damage. The market includes various types of coatings such as silicone, parylene, acrylic, urethane, epoxy, and others. Silicone coatings provide high flexibility and resistance to moisture and chemicals. Parylene coatings offer excellent dielectric properties and conformability. Acrylic coatings are cost-effective and provide good moisture and abrasion resistance. Urethane coatings offer superior chemical resistance and insulation. Epoxy coatings provide excellent protection against moisture and chemicals. Other coatings may include UV curable or tin-based options, each with their own unique properties and applications.</p></p>
