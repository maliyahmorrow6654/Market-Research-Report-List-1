<p><p>The market for Yttrium Sputtering Targets can be categorized into two types: Rotary Type and Non-Rotatable Type. Rotary Type targets are designed to spin during the sputtering process, ensuring a more uniform erosion and deposition on the target surface. On the other hand, Non-Rotatable Type targets remain stationary throughout the process. The selection between these types depends on the specific requirements of the sputtering application, with Rotary Type offering advantages of better erosion control and Non-Rotatable Type being more suitable for certain applications that demand target stability.</p></p>
