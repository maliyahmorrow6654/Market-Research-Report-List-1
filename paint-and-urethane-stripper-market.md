<p><p>Paint and urethane strippers are chemical products used for removing paint and coatings from surfaces. They are available in different market types, namely alkaline and acidic. Alkaline strippers work by breaking down the chemical bonds of paint through the use of strong alkaline detergents. On the other hand, acidic strippers utilize acids to dissolve paint and coatings. While both types effectively remove paint, alkaline strippers are gentler and less toxic compared to acidic strippers, which may be more suitable for specialized applications or heavily coated surfaces.</p></p>
