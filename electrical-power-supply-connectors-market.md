<p><strong>The Electrical Power Supply Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-In</li><li>Crimp</li><li>Push-Pull</li><li>Weld</li><li>Press-Fit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical power supply connectors market encompasses various types of connectors used to deliver electrical power in different applications. Screw-in connectors involve twisting the connector into place, offering a secure and reliable connection. Crimp connectors involve compressing the connector onto the wire, offering a strong and permanent connection. Push-pull connectors feature a mechanism for easy insertion and removal. Weld connectors involve fusing the connector to the wire, ensuring a permanent connection. Lastly, press-fit connectors involve pushing the connector into a socket or board, offering a reliable connection without soldering.</p></p>
