<p><p>The medical imaging device X-ray tube market consists of two main types: stationary anode medical X-ray tubes and rotating anode medical X-ray tubes. Stationary anode X-ray tubes are designed for low to medium X-ray output and are typically used in smaller medical imaging systems. On the other hand, rotating anode X-ray tubes are capable of high X-ray output and are commonly used in larger medical imaging systems. These tubes have a rotating target to dissipate the heat generated during X-ray production, allowing for longer exposure times and higher image quality.</p></p>
