<p><p>Automated trucks market consists of different types, including Cab Integrated Trucks and Cabless Trucks. These trucks offer flexibility to accommodate various driving conditions and can be driven both by humans and through autonomous operations. On the other hand, Cabless Trucks are fully autonomous and lack the traditional truck cabin.
