<p><p>Averaging Pitot tube is a device used to measure the flow velocity of a fluid. There are three main types: S-Shape Pitot Tubes, L-Shape Pitot Tubes, and Straight Shaped Pitot Tubes. S-Shape Pitot Tubes are curved in the middle, allowing for higher accuracy in measuring non-uniform flow. L-Shape Pitot Tubes have a single bend, making them suitable for applications where gentle bends are required. Straight Shaped Pitot Tubes have no bends, making them ideal for measuring flow in straight ducts without interference. Each type caters to specific flow measurement needs.</p></p>
