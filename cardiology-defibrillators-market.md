<p><p>The Cardiology Defibrillators Market includes different types of devices used for treating abnormal heart rhythms. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) are surgically implanted to monitor and correct irregular heartbeats. External Defibrillators are portable devices used outside the body to deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm. Wearable Cardioverter Defibrillators (WCDs) are worn by patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest and can analyze heart rhythms and deliver shocks if necessary. These devices play a crucial role in managing heart conditions and ensuring the well-being of patients with cardiac disorders.</p></p>
