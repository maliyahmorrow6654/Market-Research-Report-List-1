<p><p>The Optical Semiconductor Inspection Equipment market consists of two main types: Dark Field Inspection and Bright Field Inspection. Dark Field Inspection is a method that involves illuminating the sample with oblique light, allowing defects and irregularities to be seen as bright spots against a dark background. In contrast, Bright Field Inspection involves illuminating the sample with direct light, making defects appear as dark spots against a bright background. These techniques are commonly used in the semiconductor industry to detect and analyze imperfections and ensure the quality of semiconductor materials and devices.</p></p>
