<p><p>The tank ammunition market comprises various types of ammunition used in tanks and armored vehicles. High-Explosive Anti-Tank (HEAT) rounds are used to penetrate armored vehicles by directing the explosive energy towards the target. Armor-Piercing Fin-Stabilized Discarding Sabot (APFSDS) rounds feature a sub-caliber penetrator wrapped in a sabot, providing high velocity and armor penetration capabilities. High-Explosive Squash Head (HESH) ammunition is designed for close-range anti-tank support, utilizing a plastic explosive warhead to flatten against the target. Canister rounds contain smaller projectiles and are effective against infantry and light armored vehicles. Guided missiles offer enhanced precision and range capabilities in engaging ground targets.</p></p>
