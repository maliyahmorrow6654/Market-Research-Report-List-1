Bio-glycols, including ethylene glycol and propylene glycol, are utilized in the production of polyester fibers and coolants. D-limonene is a citrus-based solvent, while lactate esters are derived from natural fermentation. Other bio-solvents include solvents derived from soybean oil, corn oil, and other plant-based sources.</p></p>
