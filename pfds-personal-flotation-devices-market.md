Buoyancy Aids, featuring a foam core, provide extra buoyancy and are commonly used for water sports. Life Jackets are designed to turn an unconscious wearer face-up in the water. Survival Suits offer thermal protection and buoyancy in extreme conditions. Buoyancy Compensators are primarily used by divers to maintain neutral buoyancy underwater. Other PFDs include inflatable jackets, hybrid designs, and specialized models for specific activities.</p></p>
