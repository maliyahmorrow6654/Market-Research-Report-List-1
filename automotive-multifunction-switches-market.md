<p><p>Automotive Multifunction Switches are categorized into two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. The OEM market involves switches that are specifically designed and manufactured by the original automaker for installation on their vehicles during the production process. These switches are typically of high quality and ensure a seamless integration into the vehicle's overall design. On the other hand, the Aftermarket market refers to switches that are produced by third-party manufacturers and are available for purchase and installation by vehicle owners or repair shops. Aftermarket switches offer more variety and customization options but may vary in terms of quality and compatibility with different vehicle models.</p></p>
