<p><strong>The Hospital Pneumatic Tube System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Phase Pneumatic Tube Systems</li><li>Three Phase Pneumatic Tube Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hospital pneumatic tube system market consists of two types: single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase systems utilize compressed air to transport items within the hospital. These systems are commonly used for small or medium-sized hospitals due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, three-phase systems employ a more complex mechanism involving multiple vacuum pumps to deliver larger and heavier items. These systems are typically employed in larger hospitals where a higher volume of items needs to be transported efficiently and quickly.</p></p>
