<p><ul><li>Multiwall Polycarbonate Sheets</li><li>Multiwall Polycarbonate Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multiwall polycarbonate market includes two main types: multiwall polycarbonate sheets and multiwall polycarbonate panels. Multiwall polycarbonate sheets are flat, lightweight, and provide excellent thermal insulation, making them ideal for roofing and cladding applications. On the other hand, multiwall polycarbonate panels are designed with hollow chambers, offering enhanced strength and durability. These panels are commonly used for glazing, skylights, and greenhouse construction. Both types of multiwall polycarbonate offer superior transparency, UV protection, and impact resistance, making them popular choices in the construction industry.</p></p>
