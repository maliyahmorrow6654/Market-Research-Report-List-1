<p><p>Surface retarders are substances used in the construction industry to slow down the rate of concrete hardening and facilitate the creation of decorative textures and patterns on the surface. Water-based surface retarders are composed of water-soluble chemicals that delay the curing process, making it easier to wash away or remove the hardened layer. On the other hand, solvent-based surface retarders use solvents as their base, allowing for efficient penetration into the concrete and a longer working time. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific project requirements, such as climate, temperature, and desired surface texture.</p></p>
