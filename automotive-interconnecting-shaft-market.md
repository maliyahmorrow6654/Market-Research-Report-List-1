<p><ul><li>Single Piece Propeller Shaft</li><li>Multi Piece Propeller Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive interconnecting shaft market includes two types: single-piece propeller shaft and multi-piece propeller shaft. A single-piece propeller shaft is a single, solid component that connects the transmission and rear axle of a vehicle. It offers better performance and durability. On the other hand, a multi-piece propeller shaft consists of two or more interconnected sections with joints. It provides flexibility to adjust the length of the shaft, making it suitable for vehicles with different wheelbases. Both types are used in automobiles but differ in their structural design and application.</p></p>
