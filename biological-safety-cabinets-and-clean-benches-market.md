<p><p>Biological safety cabinets (BSCs) and clean benches are used in laboratories to provide safe working environments while handling hazardous materials or performing procedures that require sterile conditions. The market for these equipment can be categorized into three types: Class II Type A, Class II Type B, and Class III. Class II Type A cabinets are designed for personnel protection and sample protection but offer limited protection against hazardous vapors. Class II Type B cabinets provide protection against both particulates and vapors, while Class III cabinets are completely enclosed and offer the highest level of protection by maintaining a negative pressure environment.</p></p>
