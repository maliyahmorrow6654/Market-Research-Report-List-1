<p><p>The dye and pigment market can be categorized into various types, including disperse dyes, reactive dyes, sulfur dyes, vat dyes, and acid dyes. Disperse dyes are mainly used for coloring polyester and acrylic fibers. Reactive dyes are known for their excellent color fastness and are commonly used in dyeing cotton, wool, and nylon fibers. Sulfur dyes are used for dyeing cellulose fibers, like cotton, and offer good wash fastness. Vat dyes are applied to cellulose fibers and provide excellent lightfastness. Acid dyes are primarily used for dyeing protein fibers, such as wool and silk.</p></p>
