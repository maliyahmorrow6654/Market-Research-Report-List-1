<p><p>The Automotive Keyless Entry Systems market consists of two main types: Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). RFID technology uses radio waves to communicate between the key fob and the vehicle, allowing for convenient unlocking and starting of the car. On the other hand, BLE technology uses low-power Bluetooth connections to enable keyless entry and ignition. Both systems offer ease of use and security benefits, making them popular choices in the automotive industry.</p></p>
