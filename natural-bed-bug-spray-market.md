<p><p>The natural bed bug spray market consists of different types, including contact sprays and residual sprays. Contact sprays are designed to kill bed bugs upon direct contact, instantly eliminating the pests. These sprays are typically made from natural ingredients and are often used as a spot treatment. On the other hand, residual sprays are formulated to provide an extended period of protection. They leave a residue that continues to kill bed bugs even after the initial application, offering longer-lasting control. Both contact and residual sprays aim to eradicate bed bugs using natural ingredients.</p></p>
