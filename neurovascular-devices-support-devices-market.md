<p><p>Neurovascular devices support devices market includes microcatheters and microguidewires. Microcatheters are thin, flexible tubes used for delivering contrast dye or medications to specific blood vessels in the brain. They allow for precise navigation through complex blood vessels to treat conditions like aneurysms and arteriovenous malformations. On the other hand, microguidewires are thin wires with a tip that help in navigating through narrow blood vessels. They provide stability and guidance for the placement of other neurovascular devices during surgical procedures.</p></p>
