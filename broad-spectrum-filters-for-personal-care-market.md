<p><p>Broad spectrum filters for the personal care market are commonly classified into two types: water phase filters and oil phase filters. Water phase filters are designed to provide UV protection in water-based formulations, such as lotions and creams. These filters dissolve easily in water and form a protective barrier on the skin. On the other hand, oil phase filters are suitable for oil-based formulations like sunscreens. They disperse evenly in oils, ensuring wide-ranging UV coverage. Both water phase and oil phase filters are crucial components in personal care products to shield the skin from harmful UV radiation.</p></p>
