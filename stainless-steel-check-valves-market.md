<p><ul><li>Y-Pattern Design</li><li>T-Pattern Design</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel check valves come in two main types: Y-pattern design and T-pattern design. Y-pattern check valves have a Y-shaped body and are ideal for high-pressure applications. They offer low-pressure drop, easy maintenance, and superior flow characteristics. On the other hand, T-pattern check valves have a T-shaped body and are suitable for low-pressure applications. They provide excellent sealing but may have higher pressure drop. Both types are widely used in industries where preventing backflow and regulating flow direction are crucial.</p></p>
