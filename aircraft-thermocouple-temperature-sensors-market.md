<p><p>Aircraft thermocouple temperature sensors are devices used to measure temperature in aircraft engines and other critical components. The temperature difference between the sensing junction and the other end of the wires generates a small voltage, which is measured and converted into a temperature reading.</p><p>The aircraft thermocouple temperature sensors market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period.
