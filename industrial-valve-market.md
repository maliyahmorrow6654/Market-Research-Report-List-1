<p><p>The industrial valve market comprises various types, including gate valves, globe valves, ball valves, buffer valves, plug valves, and others. Gate valves regulate the flow of fluids through a pipe by raising or lowering a gate; globe valves control flow by a movable disk. Ball valves have a rotating ball with a hole to regulate flow. Buffer valves reduce pressure fluctuations, while plug valves use a cylindrical or conically tapered plug to control flow. Other types include butterfly valves, check valves, and diaphragm valves, each designed for specific applications and industries.</p></p>
