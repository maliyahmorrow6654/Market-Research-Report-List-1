<p><p>Thermoset powder coating market consists of two major types: general powder coating and functional powder coating. General powder coating refers to the application of a thin and protective layer on surfaces, such as metal, for aesthetic purposes, offering durability, and enhancing the appearance. On the other hand, functional powder coating provides additional properties beyond visual appeal, such as anti-microbial or anti-corrosion capabilities, making it suitable for specialized applications like food processing equipment or outdoor structures. These types serve different purposes but are both used to enhance and protect surfaces.</p></p>
