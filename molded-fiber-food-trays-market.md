<p><p>The Molded Fiber Food Trays market consists of four types: Type-1 Thick-Wall, Type-2 Transfer, Type-3 Thermoformed (Thin-wall), and Type-4 Processed. Type-1 Thick-Wall trays are made with a heavy-duty design, providing sturdiness and protection for food items during transportation and storage. Type-2 Transfer trays are lightweight and ideal for transferring food from one place to another, ensuring convenience and ease. Type-3 Thermoformed trays are thin-walled and are commonly used for packaging and displaying food products. Lastly, Type-4 Processed trays are specially engineered for high-speed processing lines, allowing for efficient automated filling and sealing operations.</p></p>
