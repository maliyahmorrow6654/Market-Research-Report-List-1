<p><p>Interlayer films are used in various industries, mainly in the construction and automotive sectors. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) interlayer films provide excellent transparency, adhesion, and impact resistance. They are commonly used in laminated glass for buildings and automobiles. PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) interlayer films have good optical clarity and adhesion, making them suitable for architectural glazing and automotive windshields. SGP (Structural Glazing Polymer) interlayer films excel in strength and durability, making them ideal for structural glazing applications in high-rise buildings and skylights. Each type of interlayer film caters to specific requirements in different applications.</p></p>
