<p><p>Chrome flour, also known as chromic oxide, is a fine green powder that is primarily used as a pigment in various industries. The ceramics industry, in particular, is a major consumer of chrome flour, as it is used to impart green color to ceramic tiles, sanitaryware, and other products.
