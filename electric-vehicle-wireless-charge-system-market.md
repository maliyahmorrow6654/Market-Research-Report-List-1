<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wireless Charge System market is divided into two main types: Magnetic Resonance Technology and Inductive Charging Technology. Magnetic Resonance Technology enables charging by transmitting energy wirelessly between coils placed on the ground and underneath the vehicle, ensuring a more efficient and flexible charging experience. On the other hand, Inductive Charging Technology uses a charging pad connected to a power source, and the vehicle is charged by placing it on the pad. Both technologies offer convenient and reliable solutions for wirelessly charging electric vehicles.</p></p>
