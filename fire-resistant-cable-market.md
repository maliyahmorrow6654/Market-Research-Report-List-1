<p><p>There are various types of fire-resistant cables available in the market. LSOH (Low Smoke Zero Halogen) cables are designed to emit minimal smoke and no toxic halogens during a fire. UL Series cables meet the safety standards set by Underwriters Laboratories. CMP, CMR, CM, CMG, and CMX are different fire-resistance ratings. CMP cables are suitable for plenum spaces, CMR for riser spaces, CM for general use, CMG for vertical tray applications, and CMX for limited use. These cables offer different levels of fire protection depending on where they are installed.</p></p>
