<p><strong>The Specialty Optical Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty optical fibers market can be categorized into two types: multi-mode specialty optical fiber and single-mode specialty optical fiber. Multi-mode fibers are designed to carry multiple light rays simultaneously, which allows for higher transmission capacity but lower fidelity over short distances. On the other hand, single-mode fibers transmit a single ray of light at a time, ensuring higher fidelity and longer transmission distances. Both types of fibers are used in various applications, such as telecommunications, medical devices, and data centers, based on their specific characteristics and requirements.</p></p>
