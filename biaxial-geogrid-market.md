<p><p>Biaxial geogrid market consists of three types: extruded, knitted/woven, and bonded. Extruded geogrids are made through an extrusion process where polymer materials are formed into a grid structure. Knitted/woven geogrids are created through a knitting or weaving process, resulting in a flexible grid structure. Bonded geogrids are formed by bonding multiple layers of geogrid materials together. These types of geogrids serve different purposes and are used in various applications such as soil stabilization, reinforcement, and erosion control in construction and civil engineering projects.</p></p>
