<p><p>Soap colorants are used to add vibrant hues to soaps, creating visually appealing products. There are two main types of soap colorants: oil-based and water-based. Oil-based colorants are often made from pigments dispersed in oil, allowing for easy incorporation into oil-based soap formulations. On the other hand, water-based colorants are typically made from water-soluble dyes and are suitable for water-based soap formulations. Both types offer a wide range of color options, allowing soap makers to experiment and create unique and attractive soaps.</p></p>
