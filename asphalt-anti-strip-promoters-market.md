<p><p>The Asphalt Anti-strip Promoters market includes two types of products: Amine Anti-Stripping Agents and Amine-free Anti-stripping Agents. Amine Anti-Stripping Agents are chemicals that contain a reactive amine group, which helps in preventing stripping or detachment of the asphalt binder from aggregates in asphalt mixtures.
