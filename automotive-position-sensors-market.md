<p><p>Automotive position sensors are devices used in vehicles to provide real-time information about the position and movement of various components. There are three main types of position sensors in the market. Multi-axis sensors are capable of measuring position in multiple directions, enabling precise tracking of a vehicle's movement. Angular sensors measure the angle of rotation of a component, such as a steering wheel. Linear sensors, on the other hand, measure linear displacement or the distance traveled by a component, assisting in tracking the movement of objects like throttle pedals or brake cylinders.</p></p>
