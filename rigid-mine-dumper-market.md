<p><p>Rigid mine dumpers are categorized into two market types: mechanical drive dumpers and electric drive dumpers. Mechanical drive dumpers are equipped with a mechanical power transmission system, which usually involves a diesel engine and a mechanical gearbox. These dumpers are generally known for their robustness and high torque output. On the other hand, electric drive dumpers use electric motors to power their wheels, making them more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. They often rely on rechargeable battery packs or electric power supplied from an external source.</p></p>
