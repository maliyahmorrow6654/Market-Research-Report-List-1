<p><p>The thin light box market is comprised of three main types: LED, EEFL, and T4 fluorescent bulb. LED (light-emitting diode) light boxes are energy-efficient and provide bright and even illumination. EEFL (external electrode fluorescent lamp) light boxes use a tube-shaped lamp for illumination, offering high brightness and long lifespan. T4 fluorescent bulb light boxes use compact fluorescent lamps, known for their energy efficiency and long-lasting performance. These different types cater to various lighting needs, allowing businesses and individuals to choose the best option based on their requirements.</p></p>
