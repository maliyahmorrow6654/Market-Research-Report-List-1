<p><p>The standard vapor chamber market consists of two main types: copper/copper fins and copper/aluminum fins. In the copper/copper fins market, the vapor chamber is constructed with copper material for both the chamber and the fins. This design allows for efficient heat transfer due to copper's high thermal conductivity. On the other hand, in the copper/aluminum fins market, the vapor chamber is made with copper while the fins are made with aluminum. This combination is chosen to balance cost and performance as aluminum is less expensive than copper but still offers good thermal conductivity.</p></p>
