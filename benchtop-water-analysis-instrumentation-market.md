<p><p>The benchtop water analysis instrumentation market includes two types of assays: physical assay and chemical assay. Physical assay involves measuring the physical properties of water samples, such as temperature, pH, conductivity, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen levels. Chemical assay, on the other hand, focuses on analyzing the chemical composition of water, including the presence of various contaminants, such as heavy metals, pesticides, bacteria, and organic compounds. Both types of assays provide crucial information about the quality and safety of water, enabling researchers, scientists, and water treatment professionals to assess and monitor water conditions accurately.</p></p>
