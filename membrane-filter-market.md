<p><p>The membrane filter market includes various types of filters, such as inorganic membrane filters, polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) filters, polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) filters, polyethersulfone (PES) filters, and others. Inorganic membrane filters are made from inorganic materials like alumina or titanium oxide and are known for their high chemical resistance. PVDF filters are made from a synthetic polymer with excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. PTFE filters are known for their exceptional chemical compatibility and high operating temperatures. PES filters offer a combination of high flow rates and low protein binding capacity. The "others" category includes various membrane filter types not mentioned specifically, such as cellulose acetate or nylon filters.</p></p>
