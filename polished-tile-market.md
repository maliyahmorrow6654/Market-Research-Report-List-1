<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Polished tile market types include stain bleeding, multi-tube blanking, microlite, and submicron powder types. Stain bleeding type tiles have a thin top layer that can bleed color when exposed to liquids, making them susceptible to staining. Multi-tube blanking type tiles are made by filling multiple tubes with different colored materials and then blanking them into a tile shape, creating a unique and varied pattern. Microlite type tiles incorporate small glass beads into the surface, giving them a shimmering effect. Submicron powder type tiles use fine powders in their production process, resulting in a smooth and glossy finish.</p></p>
