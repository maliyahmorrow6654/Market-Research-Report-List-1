<p><p>The Linear Queue Management System market encompasses various types of queue management systems. Structured Queue refers to a well-organized and orderly queue system, typically with numbered tickets or digital displays. Unstructured Queue, on the other hand, lacks a specific order, often seen at retail stores. Kiosk-Based Queue utilizes self-service kiosks for customers to take a ticket or schedule appointments. Moving Queue involves a virtual queue where customers can join remotely and receive updates on their turn in line. These different types cater to different industries and customer needs in managing queues efficiently.</p></p>
