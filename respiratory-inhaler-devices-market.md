<p><p>The Respiratory Inhaler Devices Market consists of three main types: Dry Powder Inhaler (DPI), Metered Dose Inhaler (MDI), and Nebulizer. DPIs deliver medication in dry powder form to the lungs, requiring a deep inhalation to activate the device. MDIs release a specific dose of medication in aerosol form, requiring coordination between inhalation and actuation. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a fine mist, allowing it to be inhaled through a mouthpiece or mask. These inhaler devices are used to effectively deliver medication for respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>
