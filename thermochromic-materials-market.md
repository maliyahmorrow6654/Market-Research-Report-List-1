<p><p>Thermochromic materials are substances that change color in response to temperature variations. They find applications in various industries, such as textiles, paints, coatings, and printing inks. The market for thermochromic materials can be classified into two types: reversible and irreversible thermochromic materials. Reversible thermochromic materials undergo color changes repeatedly when exposed to temperature fluctuations, allowing for multiple uses. On the other hand, irreversible thermochromic materials change color permanently after being exposed to a specific temperature, making them suitable for applications requiring a one-time color change.</p></p>
