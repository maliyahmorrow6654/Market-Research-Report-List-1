In lithography machines, they play a crucial role in removing unwanted gases and contaminants to ensure precise patterning. Similarly, in etching machines, molecular pumps evacuate reaction by-products and gases for accurate etching. In ion implantation, these pumps create a vacuum environment for precise ion beam implantation.
