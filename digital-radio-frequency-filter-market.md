<p><p>The Digital Radio Frequency Filter market offers various types of filters to meet different needs. The Low Pass Type filter allows frequencies below a certain cutoff point to pass and blocks higher frequencies. The High Pass Type filter does the opposite, letting higher frequencies pass and blocking lower ones. The Bandpass Type filter only permits a specific range of frequencies to pass through. Lastly, the Tunable Type filter allows users to adjust the frequency range that is allowed to pass, offering flexibility and customization options.</p></p>
