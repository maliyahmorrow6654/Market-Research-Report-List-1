Partial arc scales are used for applications where rotation occurs within a limited arc, such as in certain machine tools. Rotary scales, on the other hand, measure the rotation of objects along a complete circle. These different types of scales cater to specific needs, allowing for accurate and reliable position sensing in various industrial applications.</p></p>
