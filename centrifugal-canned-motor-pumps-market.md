<p><p>Centrifugal canned motor pumps are used in various applications and can be classified into different types based on their specifications. Standard basic pumps are the most common type, used in general pumping operations. Reverse circulation pumps are specifically designed to handle fluids with high solids content. High temperature pumps are capable of pumping fluids at elevated temperatures. Self-priming pumps are designed to automatically remove air from the suction line. Multistage pumps have multiple impellers for high-pressure applications. Lastly, there are other specialized pumps available for specific purposes within the centrifugal canned motor pumps market.</p></p>
