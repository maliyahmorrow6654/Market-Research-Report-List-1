<p><p>The IoT IO-Link Solutions market consists of three main types: IO-Link Master, IO-Link Sensors, and Others. An IO-Link Master is a communication device that connects various IO-Link Sensors to the central control system, allowing for data exchange and smart functionality. IO-Link Sensors are devices that collect data from the physical environment, such as temperature or pressure, and send it to the IO-Link Master. The Others category encompasses additional components and accessories that are integral to the functioning of IO-Link Solutions but do not fall into the Master or Sensor categories.</p></p>
