<p><h1>Gravure Inks Market Insights, Market Players and Forecast Till 2030</h1></p><p><strong>Market Overview and Report Coverage</strong></p>
<p><p>Gravure inks are a type of printing ink that are specifically formulated for use in the gravure printing process. Gravure printing uses a cylindrical printing plate with engraved cells, which are then filled with ink. When the plate is rotated and pressed onto the substrate, the ink is transferred to create the desired image or text. Gravure inks are known for their excellent print quality, high color strength, and fast drying time.</p><p>The future outlook for the gravure inks market appears to be promising. The growing demand for high-quality printing, especially in the packaging industry, is driving the market growth. Gravure inks have a wide range of applications, including flexible packaging, labels, magazines, and catalogs. With an increasing global population and rising disposable incomes, there is a growing demand for packaged goods, which in turn fuels the demand for gravure printing and gravure inks.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements in the gravure printing process are further driving the market growth. The development of advanced gravure cylinders and improved ink formulations are enhancing the printing capabilities and efficiency, providing additional opportunities for the gravure inks market.</p><p>The current outlook for the gravure inks market is positive as well. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecasted period. Factors such as the rapid growth of the packaging industry, increasing demand for high-quality printing, and technological advancements in the gravure printing process are expected to propel the market growth.</p><p>However, the gravure inks market faces some challenges as well. Environmental concerns regarding the use of solvent-based inks in gravure printing have led to the development and adoption of water-based and UV-curable inks. These alternatives are eco-friendly and offer lower VOC emissions. The shift towards sustainable and eco-friendly inks may impact the demand for traditional solvent-based gravure inks.</p><p>In conclusion, the gravure inks market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years due to the growing demand for high-quality printing in various industries, technological advancements, and the need for sustainable printing solutions.</p></p>

<p><p>The gravure ink market can be categorized into four main types: water-based, alcoholic, benzene-based, and petrol-based inks. Water-based gravure inks are environmentally friendly and commonly used for food packaging. Alcoholic inks dry quickly and are suitable for high-speed printing. Benzene inks provide excellent print quality but are being phased out due to health concerns. Petrol-based inks are versatile, used in a wide range of applications including magazines and labels. Each type of ink has its own characteristics and applications, catering to diverse printing needs.</p></p>
