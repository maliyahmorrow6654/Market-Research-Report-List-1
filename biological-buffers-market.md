<p><ul><li>Phosphates Type</li><li>Acetates Type</li><li>TRIS Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biological buffers are essential in maintaining the pH balance in biological systems. Phosphates type buffers, such as sodium phosphate, are commonly used in molecular biology and biochemistry applications. Acetates type buffers, like sodium acetate, are typically utilized in molecular biology and chromatography techniques. TRIS (tris(hydroxymethyl)aminomethane) type buffers are widely employed in molecular biology, electrophoresis, and protein purification, due to their ability to maintain stable pH levels. Lastly, 'Others' category includes various buffers such as citrates, carbonates, and MES (2-(N-morpholino)ethanesulfonic acid), used for specific applications depending on their pH range compatibility and reactivity with other components.</p></p>
