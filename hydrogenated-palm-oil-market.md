<p><p>Hydrogenated palm oil market encompasses two main types: fully hydrogenated palm oil and partially hydrogenated palm oil. Fully hydrogenated palm oil is produced by the complete saturation of palm oil with hydrogen, resulting in a solid fat. It has a high melting point and is widely used in food products such as bakery and confectionery items. Partially hydrogenated palm oil, on the other hand, is only partially saturated with hydrogen. It possesses a semi-solid texture and is utilized in the manufacturing of margarines, snack foods, and various processed food products.</p></p>
