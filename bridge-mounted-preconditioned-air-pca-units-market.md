<p><p>Bridge-mounted Preconditioned Air (PCA) Units are used to supply temperature-controlled air to parked aircrafts. There are two types of PCA units in the market - Engine and Electric. Engine-mounted units are powered by the aircraft's engines, utilizing bleed air to heat or cool the air supplied to the aircraft.
