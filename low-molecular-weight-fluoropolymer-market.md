<p><p>The Low Molecular Weight Fluoropolymer market encompasses three main types: Electrochemical Synthesis, Telomerization, and Oligomerization. Electrochemical Synthesis involves the production of fluoropolymers through electrolysis, wherein an electric current is used to convert chemical substances into desired products. Telomerization involves the addition of telogen molecules to perfluoroolefin monomers, resulting in the synthesis of low molecular weight fluoropolymers. Oligomerization refers to the process of combining smaller monomers into larger units through chemical reactions, creating low molecular weight fluoropolymers. These methods play significant roles in producing different types of low molecular weight fluoropolymers in the market.</p></p>
