<p><p>The organic fruit and vegetable seeds market includes different types of seeds, namely conventional seeds and hybrid seeds. Conventional seeds refer to the seeds that are cultivated using conventional methods without any genetic modification. These seeds are open-pollinated and can be used for multiple seasons. On the other hand, hybrid seeds are a result of cross-breeding between different plants to enhance specific traits. They are specifically bred to have desirable characteristics such as disease resistance or improved yield. Hybrid seeds are not suitable for saving and replanting as their traits are not stable.</p></p>
