<p><p>The Rapid Detection Kit for Veterinary Infectious Diseases market includes two main types of tests: antibody detection and antigen detection. Antibody detection tests identify the presence of antibodies in an animal's blood, indicating if the animal has been exposed to a specific infectious disease in the past. Antigen detection tests, on the other hand, detect the presence of specific proteins or particles from the infectious agent, indicating if the animal is currently infected. Both types of tests are crucial for quick and accurate diagnosis of veterinary infectious diseases.</p></p>
