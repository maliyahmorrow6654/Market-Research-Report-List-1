<p><p>The carbon fiber prepreg market encompasses various types of resins used as binding agents for carbon fibers. Epoxy resins are widely used due to their excellent mechanical properties and compatibility with carbon fibers. Phenolic resins offer high thermal stability and fire resistance. Bismaleimide (BMI) resins provide exceptional heat and chemical resistance. Cyanate ester resins offer good electrical properties and toughness. Thermoplastic resins allow for reshaping and recycling. Lastly, there are other resin types tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
