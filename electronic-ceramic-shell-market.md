<p><p>The Electronic Ceramic Shell market consists of two types: With-pin and Without-pin. With-pin shells have small metal pins inserted into the ceramic material, allowing for electrical connections to be made. These shells are commonly used in electronics applications that require electrical connections. On the other hand, Without-pin shells do not have any metal pins and are used primarily for insulation and protection purposes. They are suitable for applications where electrical connectivity is not required.</p></p>
