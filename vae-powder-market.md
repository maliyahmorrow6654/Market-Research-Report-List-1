<p><p>VAE Powder (Vinyl Acetate Ethylene Powder) is a copolymer of vinyl acetate (VA) and ethylene (E) produced via the emulsion polymerization process. VAE powder offers excellent adhesion, flexibility, and water resistance, making it highly versatile and suitable for different applications.</p><p>The VAE Powder Market is witnessing steady growth and is expected to continue its upward trajectory during the forecast period.
