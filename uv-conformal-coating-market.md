<p><p>UV conformal coating is a protective film or coating that is applied to electronic circuit boards and other sensitive components to protect them from environmental factors such as moisture, dust, and chemicals. As electronic devices become smaller and more compact, the need for effective protection against environmental factors becomes even more crucial. Manufacturers are investing in research and development activities to develop advanced UV conformal coating materials that offer better protection and performance.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising demand for eco-friendly and sustainable solutions is expected to drive the adoption of UV conformal coating in the coming years.
