<p><p>The market for Automatic Vehicle Undercarriage Inspection Systems can be broadly categorized into two types: fixed and mobile. </p><p>Fixed systems refer to undercarriage inspection setups that are permanently installed in a particular location, such as at entry points of high-security areas like airports or government buildings. These systems employ advanced imaging technology to thoroughly scan the underside of vehicles passing over them and automatically detect any potential threats or anomalies.</p><p>On the other hand, mobile systems are designed to be portable and can be easily transported to various locations as needed. These systems offer the flexibility to conduct undercarriage inspections in temporary or remote locations, such as event venues, construction sites, or checkpoints.</p><p>Both fixed and mobile systems play a crucial role in enhancing security measures by swiftly identifying any suspicious objects or modifications on vehicle undercarriages, ensuring effective safety measures are implemented.</p></p>
