<p><p>Phase Change Materials (PCMs) are substances that can store and release large amounts of thermal energy when they undergo a phase transition. The PCM market can be categorized into three types: organic, inorganic, and bio-based. </p><p>Organic PCMs are derived from hydrocarbons and offer high energy storage capacity. Inorganic PCMs, such as salt hydrates and metals, have superior thermal conductivity but lower energy storage capacity. Bio-based PCMs are eco-friendly, made from renewable sources like plant oils and fatty acids. They provide moderate energy storage capacity and are gaining popularity due to their sustainability characteristics.</p></p>
