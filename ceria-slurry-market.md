<p><p>Ceria slurry, used in chemical mechanical polishing (CMP) applications, is available in two main market types: micron grade and nano grade. Micron grade ceria slurry consists of particles that are larger in size, typically in the range of a few micrometers. This type is used for rough polishing and removal of surface imperfections. Nano grade ceria slurry, on the other hand, contains much smaller particles measured in nanometers. It is specifically formulated for fine polishing, providing a smoother and more precise finish on various surfaces.</p></p>
