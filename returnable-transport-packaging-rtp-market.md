<p><p>Returnable Transport Packaging (RTP) refers to a type of packaging system that can be reused multiple times for transporting products from one location to another. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing focus on sustainable packaging solutions. RTP helps to reduce waste generation and carbon footprint as compared to traditional packaging methods, making it an attractive option for companies looking to improve their environmental credentials.</p><p>Additionally, the rising demand for efficient supply chain management solutions is also fueling the demand for RTP. Returnable packaging offers advantages such as improved product protection, reduced transportation costs, and enhanced logistical efficiencies.
