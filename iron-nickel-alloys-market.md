<p><p>Iron-nickel alloys, also known as nickel-iron alloys, are a group of alloys that contain nickel and iron as their primary elements. Iron-nickel alloys offer high strength and corrosion resistance while being lighter compared to other materials. This makes them ideal for applications like aircraft frames, automotive components, and electrical contacts.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of renewable energy sources is also boosting the demand for iron-nickel alloys. These alloys are used in the manufacturing of wind turbines, which generate electricity from wind energy. With the increasing shift towards clean energy and the construction of new wind farms, the demand for iron-nickel alloys is expected to grow.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing electronics industry is another significant driver for the iron-nickel alloys market.
