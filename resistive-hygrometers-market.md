<p><p>Resistive hygrometers are instruments used to measure humidity levels in the air. There are two main types of resistive hygrometers in the market: relative type and absolute type. </p><p>Relative type hygrometers measure humidity levels relative to the saturation point of the air at a specific temperature. These instruments provide readings as a percentage, indicating the relative humidity in the air.</p><p>On the other hand, absolute type hygrometers measure the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, regardless of temperature. These instruments provide readings in units such as parts per million (ppm) or grams per cubic meter (g/mÂ³), providing a more precise measurement of moisture content.</p></p>
