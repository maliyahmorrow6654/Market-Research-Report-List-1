<p><p>A single beam spectrophotometer is a device used to measure the intensity of light at specific wavelengths, facilitating analysis and quantification of various substances. Single beam spectrophotometers are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, environmental analysis, and food and beverage.</p><p>The global single beam spectrophotometer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
