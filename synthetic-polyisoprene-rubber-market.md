<p><p>Synthetic polyisoprene rubber comes in two types - cis and trans. Cis polyisoprene rubber is a natural form of rubber and offers excellent elasticity, making it suitable for various applications like tires, gloves, and seals. Trans polyisoprene rubber is synthesized artificially by modifying the molecular structure of natural rubber and possesses better heat resistance than cis rubber. It finds applications in industries like automotive and aerospace, where resistance to high temperatures is essential. Both forms provide different properties, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable type for specific products.</p></p>
