<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swimming pool liners market can be classified into Type I and Type II. Type I liners are typically made from vinyl and are cost-effective options suitable for residential pools. They provide basic protection and are resistant to UV rays and pool chemicals. Type II liners, on the other hand, are made from more durable materials like fiberglass or reinforced vinyl. They are ideal for commercial pools or high-traffic areas, offering superior strength, longevity, and resistance to tear, puncture, and fading.</p></p>
