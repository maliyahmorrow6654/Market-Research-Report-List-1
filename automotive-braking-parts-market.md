<p><p>The automotive braking parts market consists of various types of components such as brake lines, disc brakes, drum brakes, brake calipers, and pistons. Brake lines are responsible for transmitting hydraulic pressure from the master cylinder to the brake calipers. Disc brakes use rotors and brake pads to generate friction and slow down the vehicle. Drum brakes use shoes and drums for the same purpose. Brake calipers hold the brake pads and exert pressure on the rotors or drums. Pistons are crucial in converting hydraulic energy into mechanical force to activate the brakes.</p></p>
