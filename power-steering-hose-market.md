<p><p>The Power Steering Hose market consists of two main types: High Pressure Power Steering Hose and Low Pressure Power Steering Hose. High Pressure Power Steering Hose is designed to handle the high pressure of the steering system and ensure smooth operation. It is typically made of durable materials such as steel or synthetic rubber. On the other hand, Low Pressure Power Steering Hose is used to transport low-pressure fluids in the power steering system. It is usually made of rubber or thermoplastic materials to ensure flexibility. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the functionality of power steering systems.</p></p>
