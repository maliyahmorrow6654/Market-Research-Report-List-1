<p><p>POM Sheet, also known as polyoxymethylene, is a type of thermoplastic material widely used in various industries. It is classified into two main market types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene refers to a market segment where POM sheets are made from a single type of polymer, resulting in a uniform material with consistent properties. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene market involves the use of multiple polymer types, resulting in POM sheets with enhanced properties such as increased impact resistance or improved chemical stability. Both market types cater to different industry requirements and offer versatile applications for various manufacturing needs.</p></p>
