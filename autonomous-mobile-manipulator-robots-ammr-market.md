Omni-directional manipulators possess the ability to move in any direction, making them versatile in navigating complex environments. On the other hand, differential manipulators can move through rotation of wheels on opposite sides, providing more stability and precision. Both types cater to different needs, offering a wide range of applications in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
