<p><p>The Veterinary Dental Handpieces Market comprises three main types: electric powered, air powered, and ultrasonic powered. Electric powered handpieces are driven by electric motors, providing consistent and precise control during dental procedures. Air powered handpieces utilize compressed air to rotate the dental bur, offering high-speed cutting capabilities. Ultrasonic powered handpieces use ultrasonic waves to create rapid vibrations, facilitating efficient tartar and calculus removal. Each type offers unique advantages and is used based on specific requirements and preferences of veterinarians and dental professionals.</p></p>
