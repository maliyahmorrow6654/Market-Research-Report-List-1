<p><p>Plastic squeeze tubes are widely used in various industries for packaging and dispensing products. There are two main types of plastic squeeze tubes: extruded plastic tubes and plastic laminate tubes. Extruded plastic tubes are made by pushing molten plastic through a die to form the tube shape. They are cost-effective and commonly used for products like toothpaste and creams. On the other hand, plastic laminate tubes consist of multiple layers with a plastic barrier that prevents leakage and provides better protection for products like pharmaceuticals and cosmetics.</p></p>
