<p><p>Direct Bonded Copper (DBC) substrate is a type of printed circuit board (PCB) technology that is widely used in power electronics applications. It is made by sandwiching a copper layer between two ceramic layers, typically aluminum oxide (Al2O3) or aluminum nitride (AlN). DBC substrates offer high thermal conductivity, excellent electrical insulation, and low thermal expansion coefficient.</p><p>The market analysis of the direct bonded copper substrate market indicates a steady growth trajectory in the coming years.
