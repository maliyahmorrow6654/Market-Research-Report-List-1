<p><p>The Mass Air Flow Sensor market is divided into four main types: Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor, Hot Film Air Flow Sensor, Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor, and Vane Air Flow Sensor. A Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor uses a heated wire to measure the amount of air entering an engine. A Hot Film Air Flow Sensor functions similarly but uses a thin film instead. A Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor determines air flow by detecting the frequency of vortices in a tube. Lastly, a Vane Air Flow Sensor estimates air flow based on the angle of a vane.</p></p>
