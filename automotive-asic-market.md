<p><strong>The Automotive ASIC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Cell Based ASIC</li><li>Gate Array Based ASIC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive Application-Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) market comprises two main types: Standard Cell Based ASIC and Gate Array Based ASIC. Standard Cell Based ASIC refers to chips designed by assembling pre-designed standard cells, offering customization options, flexibility, and higher performance. Gate Array Based ASICs, on the other hand, involve a pre-fabricated chip with a fixed gate layout that can be customized through metal layer processing. These designs provide cost effectiveness, shorter development time, and are well-suited for high-volume production in the automotive industry.</p></p>
