<p><strong>The Spray Polyurethane Foam Insulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two component open-cell foam (ocSPF)</li><li>Two component closed-cell foam (ccSPF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam Insulation (SPFI) market consists of two main types: Two component open-cell foam (ocSPF) and two component closed-cell foam (ccSPF). ocSPF is a low-density insulation that expands upon application, forming interconnected open cells. It provides excellent soundproofing and is often used for interior applications. On the other hand, ccSPF is high-density insulation that forms a sealed barrier with closed cells. It provides superior insulation and moisture resistance, making it suitable for both interior and exterior applications.</p></p>
