<p><strong>The Custom DNA Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amplicon-based Approach</li><li>Hybridization-based Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The custom DNA panel market is categorized into two types: amplicon-based approach and hybridization-based approach. Amplicon-based approach involves amplifying specific target regions of DNA and then sequencing them, allowing for targeted analysis of desired genetic variations. Hybridization-based approach, on the other hand, utilizes DNA probes that bind to the targeted regions of interest, enabling the identification of specific genetic variants. Both types provide researchers with a customizable and efficient way to study specific genomic regions of interest.</p></p>
