<p><p>The aviation lighting market can be categorized into two types: aircraft interior lighting and aircraft exterior lighting. It aims to provide a comfortable and safe environment for passengers during the flight. On the other hand, aircraft exterior lighting encompasses lights used for navigation, anti-collision, and ground operations.
