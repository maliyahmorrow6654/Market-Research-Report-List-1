<p><p>The agrochemical adjuvants market includes different types of adjuvants, namely alkoxylates, sulfonates, and silicones. Alkoxylates are additives that are created by reacting alcohols, such as ethylene or propylene oxide, with various compounds. Sulfonates are derived from sulfonic acids and are used as surfactants to enhance the performance of pesticides. Silicones are organic compounds that provide spreading, wetting, and sticking properties to the agrochemical formulation. These different types of adjuvants play a vital role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of agrochemicals in various agricultural applications.</p></p>
