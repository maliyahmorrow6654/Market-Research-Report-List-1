<p><p>Stamping fluid is a type of lubricant used in industrial metal stamping processes to improve accuracy and efficiency. Water-based stamping fluids are made of a mixture of water and additives, providing cooling and lubricating properties. They are environmentally friendly but can lead to rust formation. On the other hand, oil-based stamping fluids are made of oil and additives, which offer better lubrication and prevent rust formation. However, they are less eco-friendly and require proper disposal methods. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, and their choice depends on specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
