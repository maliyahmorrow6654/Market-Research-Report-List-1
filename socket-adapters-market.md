<p><p>Socket adapters are devices used to convert the electrical characteristics of a socket to accommodate different types of plugs. The market for socket adapters can be broadly categorized into three types: 3-pin, 2-pin, and others. </p><p>3-pin socket adapters are commonly found in countries that use a three-pronged plug system. These adapters have three pins and can be used to convert sockets with a different number of pins, enabling the connection of electronic devices.</p><p>2-pin socket adapters, on the other hand, are typically used in countries that have a two-pronged plug system. These adapters have two pins and serve the same purpose as 3-pin adapters, allowing compatibility with various socket types.</p><p>The "other" market category includes socket adapters that are used for unique plug configurations, not covered by the 3-pin or 2-pin standard. They are designed to support specific types of plugs or sockets that might not be prevalent globally.</p><p>Overall, socket adapters cater to the diverse plug systems across different countries, ensuring compatibility for electronic devices across geographical boundaries.</p></p>
