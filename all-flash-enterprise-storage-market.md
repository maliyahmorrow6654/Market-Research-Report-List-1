<p><p>The All Flash Enterprise Storage market consists of two main types: All SAS SSDs and All NVMe SSDs. On the other hand, All NVMe SSDs utilize the NVMe (Non-Volatile Memory Express) protocol, designed specifically for flash storage, delivering even greater speed and lower latency. Both options enable enterprises to leverage advanced flash technology for efficient, high-speed storage solutions, tailored to meet their specific requirements.</p></p>
