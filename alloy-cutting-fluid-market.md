<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The alloy cutting fluid market is divided into two types: oil-based and water-based. Oil-based cutting fluids are made from petroleum-based oils and are ideal for heavy-duty cutting operations. They provide excellent lubrication and cooling to the cutting tools, preventing overheating and extending their lifespan. On the other hand, water-based cutting fluids are mixed with water and offer better cooling properties while reducing the environmental impact. They are commonly used for light to moderate cutting operations where the cooling effect is crucial.</p></p>
