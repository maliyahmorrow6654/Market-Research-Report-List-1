<p><ul><li>Rear Lighting</li><li>Front Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ATV lighting systems market includes two major types: rear lighting and front lighting. Rear lighting refers to the lights installed at the back of an ATV, such as taillights and brake lights, which indicate the vehicle's position and warn other drivers during braking. On the other hand, front lighting encompasses the lights at the front of an ATV, such as headlights and fog lights, which ensure clear visibility for the rider during low-light conditions or adverse weather, enhancing safety and navigation capabilities. These lighting systems contribute to overall safety and visibility while operating an ATV.</p></p>
