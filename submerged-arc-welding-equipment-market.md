<p><p>The Submerged ARC Welding Equipment market consists of several types of equipment used in the welding process. Column and booms are large structures that provide support for the welding machines and enable precise control of the welding process. Power sources or welders generate the necessary electric current for welding. Feed heads regulate the wire feed and electrode in the welding process. Controllers monitor and control various parameters to ensure optimal welding conditions.</p></p>
