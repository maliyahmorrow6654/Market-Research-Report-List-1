<p><p>Laminated glass autoclave market types refer to the different types of gases used during the manufacturing process of laminated glass. Compressed air, carbon dioxide, and nitrogen are commonly used in the autoclave system. Compressed air acts as a cooling agent and helps in maintaining the desired pressure inside the autoclave. Carbon dioxide is used as a purging gas to remove any impurities or unwanted particles. Nitrogen is employed to create an inert atmosphere, preventing oxidation and improving the overall quality of the laminated glass. These gases play crucial roles in ensuring efficient and high-quality production.</p></p>
