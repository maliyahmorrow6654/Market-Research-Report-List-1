<p><p>Ceramic balls are commonly used in various industries due to their unique properties. The market for ceramic balls can be classified into two types: inert ceramic balls and active ceramic balls. Inert ceramic balls are chemically stable materials that are often used as catalyst supports or fillers in chemical processes and thermal storage systems. On the other hand, active ceramic balls are designed with specific functionalities, such as adsorption, ion exchange, or catalytic properties, making them useful in applications like water treatment, gas purification, or pollution control.</p></p>
