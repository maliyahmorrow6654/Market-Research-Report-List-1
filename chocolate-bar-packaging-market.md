<p><p>The chocolate bar packaging market consists of two main types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging refers to materials that are sturdy and inflexible, such as plastic or cardboard boxes. It provides excellent protection for the chocolate bars and can be designed in various shapes and sizes. On the other hand, flexible packaging includes materials like plastic films or foil, which are more pliable. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the chocolate bar industry.</p></p>
