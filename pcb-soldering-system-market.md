<p><strong>The PCB Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Oven</li><li>Selective Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering system market consists of three main types: wave soldering oven, reflow oven, and selective oven. </p><p>A wave soldering oven utilizes a wave of molten solder to connect electronic components to the PCB. It is suitable for large-scale production and is known for its high speed and efficiency. </p><p>Reflow ovens, on the other hand, use controlled heat to melt solder pastes placed on the PCB, creating strong connections. This method offers precise temperature control and is often used for smaller-scale production or surface mount technology. </p><p>Selective ovens allow for targeted soldering by using various methods like infrared, hot air, or laser. They are employed when specific components require precise and localized soldering.</p></p>
