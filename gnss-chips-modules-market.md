<p><p>GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) chips and modules are the core components used in devices for navigation and positioning purposes. They find applications in various sectors, such as automotive, transportation, agriculture, consumer electronics, and defense.</p><p>The GNSS chips & modules market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for precise navigation and location-based services. Increasing integration of GNSS technology in vehicles for applications like autonomous driving and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) is fueling the market growth.
