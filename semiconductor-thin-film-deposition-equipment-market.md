<p><p>Semiconductor thin film deposition equipment is used in the semiconductor industry to deposit thin films of various materials on semiconductor substrates. PVD equipment, or physical vapor deposition equipment, utilizes a vacuum chamber and a target material that is evaporated and then condensed onto the substrate. CVD equipment, or chemical vapor deposition equipment, involves a chemical reaction that occurs on the surface of the substrate to deposit the desired material. ALD equipment, or atomic layer deposition equipment, deposits materials in a controlled and precise manner by sequentially exposing the substrate to different precursor gases.</p></p>
