<p><ul><li>Spectral Stainless Steel</li><li>PVD Coated Stainless Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colored stainless steel market types include Spectral Stainless Steel and PVD Coated Stainless Steel. Spectral Stainless Steel is achieved by coating stainless steel with a special oxide layer, resulting in a vibrant and reflective surface that changes color when viewed from different angles. PVD Coated Stainless Steel involves a physical vapor deposition process where a thin layer of material is applied to the stainless steel, offering a wide range of color options while maintaining the durability and corrosion resistance of stainless steel. Both types provide aesthetic appeal and are used in various industries for decorative purposes.</p></p>
