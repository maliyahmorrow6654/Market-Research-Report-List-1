<p><p>Smart ECG monitors are devices that measure and record the electrical activity of the heart. They are categorized into three main market types: wearable, simple and portable, and others. </p><p>Wearable ECG monitors are designed to be worn on the body, such as on the wrist or chest. They provide continuous monitoring and real-time data, allowing individuals to track their heart health during daily activities.</p><p>Simple and portable ECG monitors are compact devices that can be easily carried and used at home or on-the-go. They typically have fewer features than wearables but offer easy and convenient heart monitoring for personal use.</p><p>The "others" market category includes ECG monitors that do not fall into the wearable or simple and portable types. This may include more specialized devices used in healthcare settings or larger-scale monitoring systems used by medical professionals to analyze heart conditions.</p></p>
