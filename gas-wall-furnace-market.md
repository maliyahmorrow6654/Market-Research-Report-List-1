<p><ul><li>Noncondensing Type</li><li>Condensing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gas Wall Furnace Market consists of two main types: Noncondensing Type and Condensing Type. Noncondensing Type gas wall furnaces operate by directly venting the combustion gases to the outside, without condensing the moisture in the exhaust gas. On the other hand, Condensing Type gas wall furnaces are designed to capture and condense the moisture in the flue gas, thus maximizing energy efficiency. Both types provide heating solutions for residential and commercial spaces, but the Condensing Type is generally more efficient and environmentally friendly, although it may be costlier upfront.</p></p>
