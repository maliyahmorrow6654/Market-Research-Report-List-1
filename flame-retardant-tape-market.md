<p><p>Flame retardant tapes are used to prevent the spread of fire in various settings. Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive applied only on one side, allowing for easy application and removal. They are ideal for surface protection and bonding. On the other hand, double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive applied on both sides, making them suitable for permanent bonding and mounting. They are commonly used in construction and automotive industries. Both types of tapes play a crucial role in ensuring fire safety by providing a barrier against flames and heat.</p></p>
