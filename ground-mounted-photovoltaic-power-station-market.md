<p><p>Ground-mounted photovoltaic power stations can be categorized into two main types: crystalline silicon photovoltaic power stations and thin film photovoltaic power stations. </p><p>Crystalline silicon photovoltaic power stations use solar panels made of silicon wafers to convert sunlight into electricity. These panels are efficient and widely used in the market.</p><p>On the other hand, thin film photovoltaic power stations use a thin layer of semiconductor material to generate electricity. They are less efficient but offer advantages like flexibility and lightweight.</p><p>Both types of power stations contribute to the generation of solar energy and help reduce carbon emissions.</p></p>
