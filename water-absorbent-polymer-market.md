<p><p>Water-absorbent polymers, also known as superabsorbent polymers (SAPs), are materials capable of absorbing and retaining large amounts of water. The increasing demand for SAPs in agriculture is one of the primary factors driving the market growth. Water-absorbent polymers help retain moisture in the soil, improving crop yield and reducing irrigation requirements. With the growing need for efficient water management and sustainable agriculture, the demand for SAPs in this sector is expected to surge.</p><p>The personal hygiene and medical sectors are also major consumers of water-absorbent polymers. These polymers are used in diapers, adult incontinence products, and female hygiene products for their absorbent properties. The rising population, increasing awareness about hygiene, and improving living standards are driving the demand for such products, thus augmenting the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the construction industry is utilizing water-absorbent polymers in various applications such as concrete, mortar, and coating materials. SAPs enhance the properties of these construction materials by improving strength, reducing shrinkage, and enhancing durability.</p><p>Overall, the water-absorbent polymer market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecasted period. The market growth is driven by the increasing adoption of SAPs in agriculture, hygiene, medical, and construction sectors.
