<p><p>The Industrial Aluminum Nitride (AIN) market can be classified into three types - Direct Nitridation Method, Carbothermal Reduction Method, and Nitridation Method. In the Direct Nitridation Method, aluminum is reacted directly with nitrogen gas under high temperatures. In the Carbothermal Reduction Method, aluminum oxide is first reduced with carbon at high temperatures, followed by nitridation. In the Nitridation Method, aluminum is first nitrided with a gaseous phase containing nitrogen at elevated temperatures. These methods are employed for the production of AIN, each offering different advantages and applications within the market.</p></p>
