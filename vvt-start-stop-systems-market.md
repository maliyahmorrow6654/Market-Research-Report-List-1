<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) and Start-Stop Systems are two key technologies in the automotive industry that contribute to improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. This technology optimizes engine performance at different RPMs, resulting in improved fuel economy and enhanced power delivery. On the other hand, Start-Stop Systems automatically shut down the engine when the vehicle comes to a stop and restart it when the driver releases the brake, saving fuel by avoiding unnecessary idling.</p><p>The VVT & Start-Stop Systems Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing emphasis on stricter emission regulations and the need for improved fuel efficiency.
