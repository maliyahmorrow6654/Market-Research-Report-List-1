<p><p>The seed coating agent market consists of various types, including suspended seed coating agents, emulsions, wettable powders, and other variants. Suspended seed coating agents are liquid formulations, where the active ingredients are kept suspended in a liquid carrier. Emulsions are mixtures of oil and water, allowing for uniform application onto seeds. Wettable powders are solid formulations that dissolve easily when mixed with water, creating a suspension for seed coating.
