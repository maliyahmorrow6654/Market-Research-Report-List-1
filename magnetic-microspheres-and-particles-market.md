<p><p>The magnetic microspheres and particles market consists of three types: coated, coupled, and conjugated magnetic microspheres and particles. Coated magnetic microspheres and particles have a surface coating that provides stability and prevents aggregation. Coupled magnetic microspheres and particles are bound to ligands or biomolecules, allowing specific targeting. Conjugated magnetic microspheres and particles are attached to a fluorescent dye, enabling visualization and tracking. These different types serve various purposes in medical diagnostics, drug delivery systems, and biotechnology research.</p></p>
