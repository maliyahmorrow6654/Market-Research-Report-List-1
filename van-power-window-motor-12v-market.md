<p><p>The Van Power Window Motor (12V) market can be categorized into two main types: OEM and Aftermarket. These motors are generally of superior quality and offer a perfect fit for the specific van model. On the other hand, the Aftermarket market comprises window motors that are produced by third-party manufacturers and are typically less expensive than OEM motors. These motors may vary in terms of quality and compatibility with different van models.</p></p>
