<p><strong>The Automotive Corner Sonar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Sonar</li><li>Active Sonar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive corner sonar systems are used in vehicles to detect obstacles and assist in parking and maneuvering. Passive sonar operates by listening to the sound waves reflected off objects, providing accurate distance measurements. It is cost-effective but less accurate. Active sonar emits sound waves and analyzes the echoes to determine object positioning. It offers higher accuracy but at a higher cost. The market for automotive corner sonar systems includes both passive and active sonar types, each with their own advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
