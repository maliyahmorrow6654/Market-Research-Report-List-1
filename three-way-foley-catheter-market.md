<p><p>A Three-Way Foley Catheter is a medical device used for urinary catheterization to drain urine from the bladder in patients who are unable to empty their bladder naturally. It consists of a long, flexible tube that is inserted into the bladder through the urethra, allowing urine to flow out. The three-way design incorporates two additional openings in the catheter, which are connected to drainage bags or irrigation systems, enabling the simultaneous drainage and irrigation of the bladder.</p><p>The Three-Way Foley Catheter Market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. Manufacturers are focusing on developing catheters with improved material biocompatibility, reducing the risk of infection and tissue damage.
