<p><strong>The Pediatric Defibrillator Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Pediatric Defibrillator Electrodes</li><li>External Pediatric Defibrillator Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pediatric defibrillator electrodes are used to provide electrical therapy to children in need of cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) or defibrillation. Internal pediatric defibrillator electrodes are surgically implanted within a child's heart to deliver electric shocks directly. On the other hand, external pediatric defibrillator electrodes are placed on the child's chest or back during CPR, allowing for non-invasive delivery of electric shocks. Both types of electrodes play a crucial role in restoring a child's heartbeat and are essential components of pediatric defibrillation devices used in emergency medical settings.</p></p>
