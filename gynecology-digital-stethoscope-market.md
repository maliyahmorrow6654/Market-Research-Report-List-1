A wireless digital stethoscope operates without any physical connection to the device being examined, utilizing advanced technology to transmit sound wirelessly to a receiver. Both options provide gynecologists with the ability to accurately assess internal bodily sounds and aid in the diagnosis and monitoring of gynecological conditions.</p></p>
