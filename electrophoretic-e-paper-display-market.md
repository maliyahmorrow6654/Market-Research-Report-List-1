<p><p>Electrophoretic e-paper display, also known as electronic paper or e-ink display, is a type of electronic display technology that mimics the appearance of ink on paper. The increasing adoption of e-readers and e-books, along with the growing demand for electronic shelf labels in retail stores, is driving the market growth. One prominent trend is the development of color e-paper displays, which bring more vibrant and engaging content to e-readers and signage. With touch capabilities, these displays can now offer interactive experiences to users, enabling them to navigate through content and interact with applications.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in flexible and rollable e-paper display technology are gaining traction.
