<p><p>The bakery conditioner market consists of two main types: powder and liquid. Powder bakery conditioners are dry and easily mixable substances that help enhance the texture and shelf life of baked goods. They are typically used in small quantities and are favored for their convenience and longer shelf life. On the other hand, liquid bakery conditioners are fluid substances that provide similar benefits but require careful measurement during application. They are often preferred in commercial bakery settings for their ease of use and ability to evenly distribute throughout the dough.</p></p>
