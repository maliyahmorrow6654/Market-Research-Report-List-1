<p><p>Liquid Chemical Delivery Systems are categorized into two types: Automatic Systems and Semi-automatic Systems. Automatic Systems are designed to deliver chemical solutions in a fully automated manner, requiring minimal human intervention. They utilize sensors, controllers, and actuators to monitor and regulate the chemical delivery process, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. Semi-automatic Systems, on the other hand, involve a combination of manual and automated processes. While they offer some level of automation, they still require human involvement in certain aspects of the chemical delivery process. Both types of systems are used in various industries for safe and efficient chemical supply.</p></p>
