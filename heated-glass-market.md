<p><p>Heated glass refers to a type of technology that provides heat insulation, defrosting, and anti-fogging functions. Laminated glass is a type of heated glass that consists of two or more layers of glass with an interlayer. It is commonly used in automotive windshields and offers enhanced safety and soundproofing. On the other hand, hollow glass, also known as double-glazed or insulated glass, is another form of heated glass that is made by sealing two glass panes with a vacuum or inert gas layer in-between. This type of glass is used for thermal insulation in buildings, reducing heat loss and noise transmission.</p></p>
