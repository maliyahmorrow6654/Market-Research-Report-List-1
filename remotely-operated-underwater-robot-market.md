<p><p>A Remotely Operated Underwater Robot (ROV) is an unmanned submersible device that is controlled from the surface by a human operator. ROVs are widely used in the oil and gas industry for inspection and maintenance of offshore structures, as well as in scientific research, military operations, and deep-sea exploration.</p><p>The future outlook of the ROV market is promising.
