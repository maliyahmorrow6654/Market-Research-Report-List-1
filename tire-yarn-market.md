<p><p>The tire yarn market consists of different types, namely polyamide, polyester, and others. Polyamide tire yarn is highly preferred due to its excellent strength, elasticity, and resistance to heat and chemicals. Polyester tire yarn is known for its cost-effectiveness, outstanding abrasion resistance, and durability. Other types of tire yarn include aramid, rayon, and steel. Aramid provides superior strength and heat resistance, while rayon offers good flexibility and adhesion properties. Steel tire yarn, on the other hand, is used for reinforcing tires with high load capacities.</p></p>
