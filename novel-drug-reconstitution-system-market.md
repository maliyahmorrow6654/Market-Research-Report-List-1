<p><p>The novel drug reconstitution system market is divided into two types: bottled and bagged. In the bottled market, the drugs are stored in vials or bottles and are reconstituted by adding a diluent or solvent to the bottle, which is then mixed before administration. On the other hand, in the bagged market, the drugs are stored in a flexible plastic bag, and the diluent or solvent is added directly to the bag, which is then mixed before use.</p></p>
