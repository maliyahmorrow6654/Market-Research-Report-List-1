<p><p>The high-performance inertial sensors market consists of two types: high-performance angular rate gyro and high-performance linear accelerometer. </p><p>High-performance angular rate gyros are sensors that measure and detect the rate of rotation or angular velocity of an object. They are used in applications such as navigation systems, drones, and robotics to provide accurate and reliable motion sensing capabilities. </p><p>On the other hand, high-performance linear accelerometers measure the acceleration and changes in velocity along a straight line. This type of sensor is commonly found in automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications, where precise measurements of linear motion are crucial for safety and control purposes.</p></p>
