<p><p>Toughened glass membrane, also known as tempered glass membrane, is a type of protective glass that undergoes a heat treatment process to increase its strength and resistance to breakage. The glass is heated to a high temperature and then quickly cooled, which creates compressive stress on the surface and tensile stress on the interior. This process gives toughened glass its characteristic strength and durability.</p><p>The toughened glass membrane market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for safety glass in various industries, such as construction, automotive, electronics, and appliances, is a major factor driving the market growth.
