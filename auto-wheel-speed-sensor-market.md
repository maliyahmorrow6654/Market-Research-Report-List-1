<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Wheel Speed Sensor market can be classified into two types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type refers to a sensor that uses a hall effect to measure the speed of the wheel. It detects changes in the magnetic field and converts it into an electrical signal. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize magnets and coils to measure wheel speed. They generate electrical signals by detecting changes in the magnetic field. Both types are commonly used in the automotive industry for various applications.</p></p>
