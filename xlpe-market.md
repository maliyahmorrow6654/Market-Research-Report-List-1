<p><strong>The XLPE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silane Cross-linked</li><li>Peroxide Cross-linked</li><li>Radiation Cross-linked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The XLPE market consists of three main types of cross-linking methods: Silane Cross-linked, Peroxide Cross-linked, and Radiation Cross-linked. Silane Cross-linked XLPE is produced by adding a silane compound during the extrusion process and then cross-linked through moisture. Peroxide Cross-linked XLPE involves the addition of peroxide to the polymer, which initiates the cross-linking reaction. Radiation Cross-linked XLPE is achieved by subjecting the polymer to high-energy radiation, causing cross-linking. These different methods result in XLPE materials with varying properties and applications in industries like electrical and automotive.</p></p>
