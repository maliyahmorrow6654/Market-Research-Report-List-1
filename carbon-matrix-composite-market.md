<p><p>Carbon-matrix composite market is categorized into various types including Carbon/Carbon (C/C), SiC/SiC, and Others. C/C composites are made of carbon fibers embedded within a carbon matrix, providing high thermal resistance and exceptional mechanical properties, making them suitable for aerospace applications. SiC/SiC composites consist of silicon carbide fibers in a silicon carbide matrix, offering excellent strength, corrosion resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for nuclear reactor components. The "Others" category encompasses other composite types like C/SiC, SiC/C, and C/ZrC, which have their unique properties and applications in diverse industries like automotive and defense.</p></p>
