<p><p>The biomass for electricity generation market consists of various types of equipment used in the process. Boilers are used to burn biomass materials, such as wood or agricultural waste, to create steam. Burners are devices that ignite and consume biomass fuel directly. Turbines are machines that convert the energy from high-pressure steam into rotary motion. Generators are used to convert this rotary motion into electricity. Besides these, there are other types of equipment involved in the biomass for electricity generation market, such as feedstock handling systems and emissions control systems.</p></p>
