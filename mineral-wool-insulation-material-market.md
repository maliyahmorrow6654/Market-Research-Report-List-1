<p><p>Mineral wool insulation materials, such as glass wool and stone wool, are widely used in construction for their excellent thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Glass wool is made from recycled glass, melted and then spun into fibers, while stone wool is primarily produced from volcanic rock. Both types offer high fire resistance, durability, and are non-combustible. They are commonly used in residential and commercial buildings to reduce energy consumption, provide soundproofing, and enhance fire safety. Glass wool and stone wool are versatile and sustainable solutions for effective insulation in various construction applications.</p></p>
