<p><p>The Underground Utilities Mapping Services market offers various types of services for accurately locating underground utilities. Electromagnetic Location (EML) is a method that detects and maps buried utilities by using electromagnetic signals. Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) is another technique that uses radar pulses to identify subsurface structures. In addition to EML and GPR, there are other mapping methods available, including acoustic methods, fiber optic inspection, and GPS surveying. These techniques help identify and map the location of underground utilities to prevent damage during construction or excavation projects.</p></p>
