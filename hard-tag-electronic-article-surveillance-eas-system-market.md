<p><p>Hard Tag Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Systems are security systems used to prevent theft in retail stores. There are different types of EAS systems available in the market. </p><p>1. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) System uses radio waves to track and identify tagged items. It offers high accuracy and can be seamlessly integrated into existing inventory management systems.</p><p>2. Acoustic Magnetic (AM) Anti-Theft System utilizes magnetic fields and sound waves to detect when a tagged item passes through the system. AM systems are known for their high detection rates and low false alarms.</p><p>3. Radio Frequency (RF) System uses radio waves to detect the presence of tags on protected items. It is commonly used in apparel stores and offers a cost-effective solution for theft prevention.</p><p>4. Electromagnetic Wave (EM) Anti-Theft System operates by generating electromagnetic fields and detects changes when a tag passes through. EM systems provide excellent detection rates and are often used in libraries and supermarkets.</p><p>5. Other market options may include newer technologies such as microwave or ultrasonic systems, which have their own unique features and advantages. These systems continue to evolve to meet the changing needs of the retail industry.</p></p>
