<p><p>Helical Submerged Arc Welding (HSAW) steel pipe is a type of seamless welded steel pipe that is widely used in various industries such as oil and gas, water transmission, construction, and others. It is produced by first forming steel plates into a cylindrical shape and then welding the edges of the plate using the submerged arc welding process. This welding process ensures a high-quality and uniform weld, resulting in a strong and durable pipe.</p><p>The HSAW steel pipe market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The driving factors for this growth include increasing demand for oil and gas transportation infrastructure, rising construction activities, and the need for efficient water transmission systems. The HSAW steel pipe offers advantages such as high efficiency, durability, and cost-effectiveness, which further boost its demand in the market.</p><p>Additionally, the growing exploration and production activities in the oil and gas sector, particularly in regions such as Asia-Pacific, North America, and the Middle East, are expected to drive the demand for HSAW steel pipes.
