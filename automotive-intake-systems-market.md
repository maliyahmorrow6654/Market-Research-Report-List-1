<p><p>The automotive intake systems market includes various types such as VVT (Variable Valve Timing), CVVT (Continuous Variable Valve Timing), VVT-i (Variable Valve Timing with Intelligence), and i-VTEC (Intelligent Variable Valve Timing and Lift Electronic Control). VVT adjusts the timing of valve operation for enhanced engine performance. CVVT offers a continuously adjustable valve timing mechanism. VVT-i optimizes valve timing for improved power and efficiency. i-VTEC combines VTEC (Variable Timing and Lift Electronic Control) and VVT technologies to adjust valve timing and lift for better engine performance and fuel economy. These systems play a crucial role in optimizing engine efficiency and power output.</p></p>
