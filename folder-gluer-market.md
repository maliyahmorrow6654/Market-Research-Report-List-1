Auto folder-gluers are fully automated machines, equipped with advanced features and programmable settings, enabling high-speed and accurate folding and gluing of boxes. On the other hand, semi-auto folder-gluers require some manual intervention, such as feeding the materials and adjusting settings. They offer slightly slower production rates compared to auto folder-gluers but are more cost-effective for businesses with lower production volumes.</p></p>
