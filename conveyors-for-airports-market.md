<p><ul><li>Belt Conveyors</li><li>Roller Conveyors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conveyor market for airports consists of two main types: belt conveyors and roller conveyors. Belt conveyors are a popular choice as they use a continuous loop of material to transport luggage and other items. They offer versatility and can handle various shapes and sizes of objects. Roller conveyors, on the other hand, utilize a series of rollers to move items from one point to another. They are commonly used for sorting and accumulating luggage, providing efficient and smooth operations in airports.</p></p>
