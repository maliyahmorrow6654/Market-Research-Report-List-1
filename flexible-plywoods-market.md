<p><p>The flexible plywoods market can be classified into three types based on thickness: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywoods below 6mm in thickness are commonly used for applications that require lightweight and flexible materials, such as packaging and furniture backing. Plywoods in the 6mm-18mm range are versatile and find application in various sectors, including construction, interior decoration, and furniture manufacturing. Plywoods above 18mm are typically utilized for heavy-duty tasks like flooring and structural support. These categorizations help cater to different requirements and ensure the availability of appropriate flexible plywoods for diverse applications.</p></p>
